    • C++ with Qt

    Interests

    Driven by my passion for smartphones, I started my programming journey specifically tailored for these devices. This fascination led me to delve into the realms of programming for Blackberry 10, Android, and iOS platforms. Throughout my career, I have primarily focused on mobile development in the smartphone domain, allowing me to hone my expertise in crafting captivating user experiences.

    However, my curiosity extends beyond mobile development. I take pleasure in expanding my knowledge by reading about diverse programming languages and frameworks, many of which I have no intention of using. Constantly seeking growth, I dedicate time to understand their unique advantages and drawbacks. This perpetual quest for knowledge fuels my enthusiasm for the programming world.
